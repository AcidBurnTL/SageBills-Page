The Problem

Utility bills shouldn’t be
this hard

Most households track bills in spreadsheets, paper folders, or not at all. There’s a better way.

📄
Paper chaos

Invoices scattered across drawers, email folders, and PDF downloads. Finding last month’s electricity bill takes twenty minutes.

⚡
Surprise bills

No warning when your heating bill doubles in January. You open the invoice and the number is already overdue.

📊
No visibility

Impossible to see how much you’re spending across all utilities, let alone identify patterns or opportunities to save.
