## 2. Description of Service

SageBills is a utility bill tracking application for iOS and macOS that allows you to:

- Record and organize utility bills (electricity, gas, water, internet, and other categories)
- Scan invoices using on-device optical character recognition (OCR)
- View predictions of future utility costs based on historical data
- Detect anomalies in your billing patterns
- Set and track savings goals
- Receive local reminders for upcoming bill due dates
- Export your bill data in CSV format
- Search your bills through device Spotlight search

SageBills is a personal finance tracking tool. It does not provide financial advice, nor does it process payments to utility providers on your behalf.
